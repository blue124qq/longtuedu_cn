Investor FAQs

1)What does the Company do?

Bright Scholar is a global premier education service company, dedicated to providing quality international education to global students and equipping them with the critical academic foundation and skillsets necessary to succeed in the pursuit of higher education. Bright Scholar also complements its international offerings with Chinese government-mandated curriculum for students who wish to maintain the option of pursuing higher education in China. As of May 31, 2020, Bright Scholar operated 80 schools across ten provinces in China and eight schools overseas, covering the breadth of K-12 academic needs of its students. In the nine months ended May 31, 2020, Bright Scholar had an average of 51,970 students enrolled at its schools.


2)Where is the Company's corporate headquarters?

Bright Scholar's corporate headquarters are located at No.1,Country Garden Road, Beijiao Town, Shunde District, Foshan, Guangdong 528300,China.

3)Where is the Company incorporated?

Bright Scholar is incorporated in the Cayman Islands.

4)When was the Company found?

The Company traces its history back to Guangdong Country Garden School with the first private school founded in 1994 and began doing business as Bright Scholar in 2016.

5)Where is the Company listed and what is the ticker symbol?

Bright Scholar stock is listed in form of American Depositary Shares representing Class A Ordinary shares on the New York Stock Exchange under the ticker symbol “BEDU”.

6)Which is Bright Scholar's Depositary Bank?

Bright Scholar's Depositary Bank is The Bank of New York Mellon.

7)Does Bright Scholar pay a cash dividend?

Bright Scholar does not expect to declare or pay any dividends in the foreseeable future. The Company intends to dedicate funds and future earnings to drive operations and fuel business expansion.  

8)When is Bright Scholar fiscal year end?

Bright Scholar's fiscal year ends August 31.

9)Who is Bright Scholar's independent accountant ?

Bright Scholar's independent accounts are Deloitte ToucheTohmatsu Certified Public Accountants LLP.

10)How can I obtain financial information of Bright Scholar?

Bright Scholar posts the most recent and past quarterly earnings information on its website. To request additional investor-related information, please email: BEDU.IR@gcm.international.

11)Who makes up the Bright Scholar Management team and Board of Directors ?

Please refer to the section under Corporate Governance in which we have the details of our senior management team as well as board members.

12)Do you have another question that hasn't been answered?

If you have any other questions, please send to the IR email address: BEDU.IR@gcm.international.
