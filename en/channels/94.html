International Curricula

Bright Scholar , Bright Future

IB Curriculum

IB curriculum is authorized by the International Baccalaureate, offering standardized courses and assessments to students globally, and the diploma acquired under IB curriculum is of great value, recognized and respected by the world's top universities.

★ PYP Kindergarten for preschoolers aged 3-6

★ Primary Years Programme for school children aged 6-12

★ Middle Years Programme for middle school students aged 12-16

★ Diploma Programme for high school students aged 16-19

CIE Programmes

CIE programmes are authorized by Cambridge International Examinations, offering International Cambridge education for nearly 1,000,000 students from 10,000 Cambridge schools in 160 countries globally to fulfill their dreams of being admitted into the world's top universities

★ International General certificate of Secondary Education (IGCSE) for middle school students aged 15-16

★ General certificate of Education Advanced Level (A-Level) for high school students aged 16-19

Advanced Placement Program

The Advanced Placement Program, offered by the College Board, provides college-level courses for high school students aged 15-19. Currently, Educational Testing Service (ETS) develops exam questions and organizes the AP exams. AP now covers 20 subject groups, and 38 subject courses which symbolize the academic depth of high schools in the US.

International Foundation Diploma

IFD is a pre-university qualification developed by the Northern Consortium (NCUK) preparing the students for the UK Unionist Party and AUP Universities. It is targeted towards students aged 16-19 with the plan to major in Business Studies and Computer Science.

Additional International Curricula

Sino-Canadian Duel Diploma Program for middle school students aged 15-19

Sino-US AH Program, Sino-Australian Programme, Sino-German Programme for middle school students aged 16-19
