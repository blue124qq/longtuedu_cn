Enrollment Requirements

The majority of our students come from mainland China, Hong Kong, Macau and Taiwan, but we warmly welcome students from other countries to enroll in our schools.

Application Materials

Kindergarten

One 1-inch color photo of applicant and hukou booklet. (Home Return Permits for students from Hong Kong and Macau, MTPs for students from Taiwan and passports for students from other foreign countries.)

Primary school to High school

1. Two 1-inch color photos of applicant and hukou booklet. (Home Return Permits for students from Hong Kong and Macau, MTPs for students from Taiwan and passports for students from other foreign countries.)

2. Transcript from previous school (Except G1 students)

3. Transcript of senior high school entrance national examination for students applying to G10.

Entrance Exam and Admission Criteria

01

Kindergarten

Bring the receipt of application, and participate in the related entrance exam

Admission criteria: Healthy and normal mental development

02

Primary School

No written exam for G1-G5 students. Interview only.

Admission criteria: Future development potential, study abilities and overall qualities

03

Middle School

Written examinations for English and mathematics; interview.

Admission criteria: No less than 70 out of 100 points for each subject and interview evaluation up to level B (from A, B, C, D four levels)

04

High School

Written examinations for English, Mathematics, Physics (or Chemistry, Economics is also acceptable for students who choose international courses) and interview.

Admission criteria: No less than 75 out of 100 points for each subject and interview evaluation up to level B (from A, B, C, D four levels).

Foreign Students Application

01

Kindergarten

Bring the receipt of application, and participate in related entrance exam

Admission criteria: Healthy and normal mental development

02

For the students apply for G6-G8 international courses

Written examinations for English and Mathematics, plus interview

Admission criteria: 70 out of 100 points, and interview evaluation up to level B (from A, B, C, D four levels)

03

For the students apply for G9-G11 international courses

Written examinations for English and Mathematics, plus interview

Admission criteria: 75 out of 100 points,and interview evaluation up to level B (from A, B, C, D four levels).

04

For students applying for Chinese courses in G10-G12

HSK examination (proper grade suggestion will be based on results), plus interview

Admission criteria: 75 out of 100 points,and interview evaluation up to level B (from A, B, C, D four levels).

Acceptance Letter

Students accepted by our school should take the following files to the admissions office and finance office to register at the school.

Two guardians' ID certificates and their copy;

Bank card for tuition fee;

Vaccination certificate;

Entrance examination;

For further assistance in the application process, please contact our admissions offices.

8:00-18:00 from Monday to Friday (two shifts)

8:30-17:30 in weekends and holidays (lunch time between 12:00-13:00)

Generally, we have specialist at the admissions office every day except during Chinese Spring Festival.
