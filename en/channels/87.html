Origins Chronology

Chronology

2017

Country Garden Education Group was rebranded as "Bright Scholar Education Group" and listed on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE ticker: BEDU).

2016

Through a strategic cooperation relationship with CIE, Country Garden Education Group became registered as a Cambridge associate.

2014

Country Garden Education Group was founded.

2011

The first official authorization for PYP curriculum teaching qualification by IB.

2007

First official authorization of A-Level and IGCSE teaching qualification by Cambridge International Examination Committee (CIE).

2005

Guangdong Country Garden School was awarded "Guangdong Advanced Private School" by Department of Education of Guangdong Province.

2002

Tailored and specialized "Country Garden school-based curriculum and curriculum system" was published.

2001

The first official authorization for DP and MYP curriculum teaching qualification by the International Baccalaureate (IB).

2001

The first Guangzhou branch school was built.

1998

100% university enrollment rate for the first batch of graduates.

1994

The first school was established in Shunde, Guangdong.
