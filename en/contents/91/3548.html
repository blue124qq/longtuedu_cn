Bright Scholar Education Group Won the "Golden Bauhinia" Award of China's Securities "The Most Brand Valued Listed Company in 2019"

2019.12.24

On December 5, the highly anticipated 9th China's Securities “GoldenBauhinia” Award Presentation Ceremony was held in Hong Kong. Bright Scholar

Education Group was awarded the most brand listed Valued Company.


The event was jointly organized by the Hong Kong Ta Kung Wen Wei Media Group in conjunction with Beijing Association of Listed Companies,

Hong Kong Chinese Enterprise Association, Hong Kong China's Securities Association, Hong Kong China Finance Association, Hong Kong

Securities Association, Hong Kong Chartered Secretary Association, Hong Kong China Mergers and Acquisitions Association and others. The theme of

"70 years of magnificent waves, towards a broad stage", attracted about 600 guests from nearly 100 mainland and Hong Kong executives from large

and medium-sized listed companies. Hong Kong political and business celebrities and the Chief Executive of Hong Kong also attended.

Mrs. Carrie Lam Cheng Yuet-ngor, attended the event and shared speech.



For more than 20 years of China's capital market development, the two listed companies and their executives have been some of the largest, highest level, the most credible listed companies. China's Securities "Golden Bauhinia" Awards have been held eight times, and during each competition there has been a comprehensive

evaluation of all the outstanding characteristics of each company. Their remarkable achievements have enhanced the status of Chinese enterprises in the international

market and promote the increased economic cooperation of China. This award has been compared to the industry’s Oscar Awards.


At the opening ceremony of the award ceremony, Mr. Jiang Zaizhong, member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative

Conference(CPPCC) and chairman of the Hong Kong Ta Kung Wen Wei Media Group, delivered aspeech on behalf of the organizers. He said that

this year is the ninth consecutive year of the Golden Bauhinia awards, but also the 70th anniversary of the founding of New China. In the past 70 years,

China has achieved a great leap in economic development and China's total economic value has leapt to the second in the world. It has contributed more than 30% of the world economic growth, and the "Chinese miracle" has been left behind by the struggle of thousands of Chinese entrepreneurs. He

stressed that the Golden Bauhinia Award is not only a review of the great achievements of China over the past 70 years, but also a tribute to the

enterprises, institutions and managers who have made outstanding contributions to the economic recovery of China.



Since listing on the New York Stock Exchange in 2017, Bright Scholar has seized the opportunity to move forward, starting in Shunde, Guangdong,

radiating through the country, and accelerating the process of overseas mergers and acquisitions. It has acquired Bournemouth School, a century-old private school, BCS, St. Michael's School, the UK's leading private school, and Bosworth Independent College, the UK's leading boarding school.

In July, the acquisitionof CATS Colleges, a well-known British education group, caused a stir in the education sector as the largest overseas acquisition

of Chinese education sector.


At present, Bright Scholar has 80 schools and 19 Elan schools in 10 provinces of China, including 7 international schools, 15 bilingual schools and 58

kindergartens, and 8 overseas K12 schools and 10 overseas language schools in the United Kingdom, the United States and Canada (the data is as of 7 November 2019). It has become the world's leading K12 education group, with more than 46,000 students around the world to provide quality

education services.


Bright Scholar's CFO Li Dongmei said that in the past 25 years, Bright Scholar has always adhered to the original heart, adhering to the founder

Yang Guoqiang's educational philosophy, committed to the country and society to cultivate students with a “Chinese Soul with Global Vision.”


In the future, Bright Scholar will continue to integrate high-quality educational resources at home and abroad with a global perspective, build a

"Bright Scholar World School", creating a global classroom, providing an immersive international learning environment, so that students and teachers

around the world can seamlessly connect across the region to carry out teaching and learning and ensure that education faces a longer future.
