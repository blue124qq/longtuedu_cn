Bright Scholar Joins Hands with Guizhou University to Help the Development of International Education in Southwest China

2019.12.24

On October 24, the signing ceremony of strategic cooperation between Bright Scholar Education Group and Guizhou University was held at Guizhou University.


Bright Scholar and Guizhou University strategic cooperation contract site (left forGuizhou University Vice President Li Junqi, right for Bright Scholar Group VicePresident Cheng Jinsheng)


Cheng Jinsheng, Vice President of Bright Scholar Education Group, and Li Junqi, Vice President of Guizhou University, signed the contract. Song Liying, General Manager of

human resource management center of Bright Scholar Education Group, Ren Kangmin, Dean of the School of Continuing Education of Guizhou University, Liu Ying, Deputy

Director of the Department of Foreign Cooperation, Jia Wensheng, Vice President of the Institute of Science and Technology Research, Zhang Jing, Deputy Director of the

Education Department, Wang Zhongyu, Deputy Director of the Department of Recruitment and Employment, Xie Mingying, Deputy Director of the Department of International Exchange and Cooperation. The signing ceremony was attended by Chen Xiaohong, head of the project department of the Bright Scholar Campus Operations Center, Liu Qin,

principal of Bright Scholar Guizhou Huaxi Country Garden International School, and Zhang Chenglin, executive principal.


Bright Scholar Education Group and Guizhou University held a signing ceremony forstrategic cooperation. They announced that the University and Bright Scholar will form an

alliance to create research centers, teacher training, teacher exchanges, internship employment, winter and summer camps and other aspects.


Bright Scholar and Guizhou University strategic cooperation contract site


During the symposium, the two sides held in-depth discussions on the implementation of cooperation matters. High-quality education and teachers are

the first priority. All along, Bright Scholar attaches great importance to the training of teachers, and this exchange focused on how to use university resources to carry out basic education teacher training and exchanges. Inaddition, the two sides also discussed cooperation in poverty alleviation in education. Bright Scholar Guizhou Huaxi Country Garden International School will be the first pilot of strategic cooperation between Bright Scholar and

Guizhou University, further integrating Guizhou University's academic resources with Bright Scholar's international school experience, and jointly

promoting the development of international education in the southwest region.


At the same time, Song Liying, general manager of the Human Resource Managemen Center of Bright Scholar Education Group, conducted a special

lecture on talent training in Guizhou University, and conducted in-depth career planning and employment consultation for students of Guizhou University.



Photo after the meeting


Guizhou University was founded in 1902, and has gone through 117 years. It is the only "double-class" construction of universities and "ministry and provinces to build" universities in Guizhou province. The school has 40 colleges, a full range of disciplines, covering 12 subjects other than the military.

The university has always been based in Guizhou, and fully serves the economic and social development of Guizhou. They have given attention to the

cultivation of the development of characteristic strains, training the talents needed by society and enterprises, focused on building scientific research

projects and platforms that can help Guizhou's economic and social development.


In September of 2012, Guizhou University is listed as one of the 14 universities in the country's "Comprehensive Strength Improvement Project of

Midwestern Universities." Then, April of 2016, it is listed as the "oneprovince, one school" national key construction university in the centra land western

regions. In February of 2018 it became the ministry of the joint construction of universities. In addition, Guizhou University's plant protection discipline

was selected as a world-class construction discipline, chemistry, plant and animal science was selected in the world's top 1% ESI discipline.


The signing was jointly organized by the Project Department of the Bright Scholar Campus Operations Center and the Department of Foreign

Cooperation of Guizhou University.
